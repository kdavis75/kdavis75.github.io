Welcome to the Rundown with Kayson and Rich !

Rich and Kayson are 2 college students pursuing careers in journalism. They're here to keep you up to date on the latest news in sports and voice their opinions while doing so.

Another week means another rundown, Rich and Kayson discuss the recent video from NFL players addressing police brutality and their reactions to the NFL's response to it.
